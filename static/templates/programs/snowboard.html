Snowboard Program

It is time to sharpen your riding skills again! This year for SoCal NSP, we have scheduled a total of 10 Snowboard Enhancement Clinics throughout Southern California. Each class will be held at a different mountain. You will ride with a America Association of Snowboard Instructors (AASI) certified Level II and/or Level III instructor/patroller. You will learn first had from the pros on proper gears, set up, ride analysis, and work on your skills to improve to the next level.

All Far West So Cal regional snowboard clinics are FREE. Please help keep these clinics at no cost and make a DONATION to help support new instructors with books for the So Cal region.
